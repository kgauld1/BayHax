About Us

Purpose:

BayHax is a handy tool for parents to look after their child's emotional health. This mood tracker is perfect for young children and/or children with disabilities who have difficulty communicating their emotions. By using BayHax, parents can get a clear picture of their child’s overall emotional health and become better-equipped to handle their child’s mood swings. For the child, BayHax can also serve as a therapeutic friend to confide in.

BayHax's name was inspired by Disney's 2014 animated feature film, "Big Hero 6" where BayMax - an inflatable marshmallow looking robot - serves as a personal healthcare companion.
